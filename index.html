About Me

I have delved into the arts sector around 4 years ago when I enrolled in a 1 year diploma course at the University of Malta. Since then I have earned the diploma in design, and I am currently reading for a degree in digital arts. In my second year I have started my own personal design company called ‘Nisga’. This opportunity could help me reach my goal and have a successful business in design, photography and filming and truly have passion and love at the heart of arts and design. Since the start of the business I have worked with worked with several clients such as Vecchia Napoli, Santa Cruz, Beer kitchen, Sigma Igaming Conference and Edward De Bono while supporting countless student organizations. I am also part of the committee of a voluntary theatre company called Arthaus managing media and marketing. I have just been nominated by LLA Accademia in Slovenia and won the Referenca Award chosen by Stane Jerko for photography potential and perseverance.
